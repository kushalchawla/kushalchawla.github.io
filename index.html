I am a PhD student (starting Fall’19) at the Institute for Creative Technologies (ICT) , University of Southern California (USC). I am advised by Gale Lucas and Jonathan Gratch. I am currently interested in the overlap between Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and Natural Language Processing (NLP), focusing on enabling agent-human negotiations through Natural Language.

Prior to USC, I pursued my Bachelors in Computer Science from the Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati (IITG). After graduating in 2017, I spent a couple of years with Adobe Research, focusing on NLP applications in marketing, including affect modelling and text summarization.
